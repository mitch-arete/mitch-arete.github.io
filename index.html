About

Areté Capital is an investment firm based in Austin, Texas with one core objective: maximize wealth creation. Fund decisions are always made with this fundamental objective in mind. The fund aims to generate absolute returns that are uncorrelated with long-term market conditions. Please contact mitch@arete-cap.com for more information.

The business targets long-only small cap stocks and liquid high yield bonds. Our work and process is research-backed and thesis-driven. We pursue rational investments in favorable situations that are poised for long-term success. Rather than force ourselves into unattractive investments, we prefer to wait for situations that check all of our long-term criteria. As a result, the portfolio is concentrated and contains high conviction, long-term investments.

The fund aligns with businesses that are poised to produce resilient and growing free cash flow over the long-term. Investing in quality, growing businesses at attractive valuations is a natural hedge to capital impairment. The portfolio maintains a growth orientation which helps drive long-term outperformance and upside.

Investment Criteria

  • Market capitalization between $50mm and $5bn
  • Attractive free cash flow characteristics and capital allocation
  • 3-yr inorganic sales CAGR expectation of 10%+
  • 3-yr inorganic EBITDA CAGR expectation of 20%+
  • EV/EBITDA ratio of <12.5x or EV/Sales ratio of <2.0x
  • Improving 5-yr competitive positioning
  • Other specific criteria

Portfolio Management

  • Target holding period 1-3 years
  • Diligence period 5+ years
  • Target investment IRR of 20%+
  • Concentrated portfolio of 10 to 20 investments
  • Portfolio of best ideas
  • Consumer, industrials, and certain technology
  • Position weightings based on conviction
  • Other specific criteria
